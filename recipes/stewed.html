Stewed Octopus

Sylke Rohrlach, CC BY-SA 4.0 , via Wikimedia Commons

Description

A nice, cold-weather octopus recipe.

Ingredients

  • octopus
  • 4T olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 1c white wine
  • 1c canned crushed tomatoes
  • 1t chili flakes
  • 1t salt
  • 2T honey
  • 2T fresh dill
  • pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Boil octopus in salt water, cooking 1 - 2 minutes.
  2. Cut octopus into large pieces. Saute in oil, 2 - 3 minutes.
  3. Add garlic, saute 2 minutes more.
  4. Add wine. Bring to boil, 3 - 4 minutes.
  5. Add tomatoes, chili flakes. Simmer.
  6. Add salt and honey. Mix, cover, simmer 30 minutes.
  7. Add dill.
  8. Before serving, uncover 10 minutes to let sauce thicken.
