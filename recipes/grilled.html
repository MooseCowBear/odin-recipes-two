Grilled Octopus

Karen from Los Angeles, USA, CC BY 2.0 , via Wikimedia Commons

Description

A simple, beginner-friendly recipe.

Ingredients

  • 2lbs octopus
  • olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • lemon juice
  • salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Put octopus in a pot and boil for 40 minutes.
  2. Remove from pot.
  3. Drizzle with oil, add garlic.
  4. Let cool for 1 1/2 hours.
  5. Heat grill to medium-high.
  6. Grill on each side.
  7. Drizzle again with oil. Add salt, pepper, lemon juice.
