Spanish Octopus

Description

Spanish-style recipe for the paprika lovers out there.

Ingredients

  • octopus
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • bay leaf
  • 1 1/2t smoked paprika
  • 1 1/2t salt
  • 4T olive oil, divided
  • 1/2c white wine
  • 1T lemon juice
  • cayenne pepper

Steps

  1. Place onion, garlic, bay leaf, paprika, salt, 1T oil into sauce pan. Cook over medium-high heat until onion softens.
  2. Add wine. Simmer.
  3. Add octopus. Cover, reduce heat to low. Simmer 20 minutes, turn octopus over. Continue cooking 40 - 45 minutes.
  4. Remove from heat. Cool.
  5. Refrigerate overnight.
  6. Cut octopus into 3 - 4 pieces. Brush with olive oil.
  7. Transfer braising liquid to sauce pan. Bring to boil, then remove from heat.
  8. Strain liquids.
  9. Stir in lemon juice, oil, salt, cayenne pepper.
  10. Grill octopus.
  11. Slice octopus. Spoon braising liquid mixture over the top.
