Octopus Ceviche

H. Zell, CC BY-SA 3.0 , via Wikimedia Commons

Description

A refreshing dish for beach picnics.

Ingredients

  • cooked octopus
  • red onion, thinly sliced
  • lime juice
  • cilantro
  • olive oil
  • salt

Steps

  1. Cut octopus into bite-sized pieces.
  2. Combine with remaining ingredients.
