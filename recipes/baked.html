Baked Octopus (with Potatoes)

Free Public Domain Illustrations by rawpixel, CC BY 2.0 , via Wikimedia Commons

Description

Another technique for cooking octopus, this time using an oven!

Ingredients

  • octopus
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 2lbs potatoes, peeled and chopped
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 5 cloves garlic
  • 4T olive oil
  • 1lb cherry tomatoes
  • 1/4c white wine
  • parsley

Steps

  1. Add octopus and bay leaves to pot of water. Cook over medium heat 15 minutes.
  2. Add potatoes, garlic, onion to baking dish. Season with salt and pepper.
  3. Place octopus on top.
  4. Cover and bake for 1 hour at 400 F.
  5. Add tomatoes and bake for 20 minutes more.
  6. Sprinkle with parsley and serve.
