Greek Octopus

A. Pollock, Public domain, via Wikimedia Commons

Description

A simple Greek-style octopus recipe.

Ingredients

  • octopus tentacles
  • 1/2c olive oil
  • 1/4c red wine vinegar
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • lemon juice
  • 2t dried oregano
  • 1/2t pepper
  • 1/2t red chili flakes

Steps

  1. Combine all ingredients except octopus to make vinaigrette.
  2. Grill octopus, 3-5 minutes each side over medium-high heat.
  3. Drizzle vinaigrette over octopus.
